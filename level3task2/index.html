Ideal Candidate Profile

We're looking for passionate individuals who are eager to learn and grow in web development.

Who Should Apply?

  • Pursuing or hold a degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • Frontend: HTML/CSS, JavaScript (React/Angular preferred)
  • Backend: Node.js, Python, or Java basics (optional but a plus)
  • Databases: SQL/NoSQL (MongoDB, PostgreSQL)
  • Version Control: Git/GitHub fundamentals
  • Soft Skills: Problem-solving, teamwork, and adaptability

What to Expect During the Internship?

Our structured 3-6 month program ensures you gain comprehensive experience.

Month 1: Onboarding & Training

Orientation to company processes and intensive training in our tech stack (React, Node.js, MongoDB).

Month 2-3: Project Assignment

Work on a real client project under mentor supervision with weekly code reviews.

Month 4-6: Advanced Work

Take ownership of features, optimize performance, and prepare for final evaluation.

Final Week: Demo & Review

Present your work to leadership team, receive feedback, and certification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship program.

Is this internship remote or on-site?

This internship is fully remote.

Do you provide stipends or housing?

Yes, we provide a competitive monthly stipend (₹15,000-₹25,000 based on skills). Housing assistance isn't provided currently.

What's the selection process?

Our process has 3 stages: 1) Resume screening, 2) Technical test (coding challenge), 3) Final interview with our engineering team.

Can I apply if I'm a beginner?

Absolutely! We welcome beginners with demonstrated passion for coding. Our training will help bridge knowledge gaps.

What are the working hours?

Standard 8-hour days with flexibility.
